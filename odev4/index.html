A moodboard is a visual representation of a design concept, theme, or idea. It is a collection of images, colors, textures, and typography that conveys the mood, style, and direction of a design project. Moodboards are commonly used in graphic design, interior design, fashion design, and web design. In this blog post, we'll guide you through the process of creating a moodboard.

    Before creating a moodboard, you need to define the theme or concept of your project. This could be a brand identity, a website redesign, or a product launch. Having a clear idea of the project's theme will guide your image selection and design choices.
